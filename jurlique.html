JURLIQUE

TypeProduct Animation
SoftwareHoudini
Duration30 seconds

A 30-second concept film for Jurlique's Rosewater Balancing Mist, built without a brief. The work starts with what Jurlique actually believes: that farming practices, lunar cycles, and biological processes are not incidental to a product. They are the product. The film tries to make that legible. Particle systems, environmental simulation, and the Bio-Intrinsic process as visual architecture.
