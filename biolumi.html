BIOLUMI

TypeFluid Simulation
SoftwareHoudini
Year2024

BioLumi is a fictional product. It doesn't exist and couldn't — not yet. The brief was self-imposed: what would a wellness product look like if its active ingredient was light itself. The film doesn't advertise anything. It prototypes something that only simulation can build, using Houdini fluid dynamics and bioluminescence as the material. An impossible object, rendered anyway.
